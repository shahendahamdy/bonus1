cookie Monster CupCakes


Cookie Monster Cupcakes will go down a treat at any little kids party and with this technique they will be done quickly without the use of any fancy equipment.
Using a small amount of butter cream ‘glue’ a brown mini m&m onto the white chocolate melt to make the eye. Fill your ziplock bag with blue butter cream and then around one corner make lots of holes with a skewer. Generously cover you cupcake with blue swirls of buttercream.
